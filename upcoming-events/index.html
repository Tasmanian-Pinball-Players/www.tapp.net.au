Upcoming events

📜 Code of Conduct: By attending TAPP events, all participants agree to abide by our guidelines.

Compete for WPPR Points!
Most TAPP events are IFPA registered and award WPPR points – contributing to both the Australian Championship Series and the international IFPA rankings. If you’re looking to climb the leaderboard or qualify for championships, these events count!

There are also occasional non-TAPP events in Tasmania that are also IFPA-sanctioned. You can find them by searching the IFPA calendar or browsing by state:
