Welcome to the TAPP Leaderboard. Here, we track the rankings of pinball players across Tasmania based on their performance in official events.<\/p>\n\n\n

How It Works<\/h2>\n\n\n

Our leaderboard uses the International Flipper Pinball Association (IFPA) points system. When you participate in IFPA-endorsed events within the state, you earn World Pinball Player Rankings (WPPR) points. These points determine your position on our state leaderboard as part of the Australian Championship Series (ACS).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

At the end of the calendar year, the top 16 players will be invited to the state finals. Winners from the state finals will qualify for the national championships.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    How the Finals Work<\/h2>\n\n\n
      \n
    • State Finals:<\/strong> Players accumulate IFPA points through various endorsed events throughout the year. The top-ranked players based on these points are eligible to compete in the state finals.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • National Finals:<\/strong> The state finals culminate in the ACS, where state champions and top performers compete for national glory.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • International Representation:<\/strong> Winners of the ACS have the opportunity to represent Australia in the IFPA World Pinball Championship, competing against the best from around the globe.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n
