The Thrill of the Leaderboard<\/h2>\n\n\n

Welcome to the Tasmanian Association of Pinball Players’ (TAPP) Leaderboard! This is where the excitement of competition meets the joy of pinball. Our leaderboard showcases the top pinball players in Tasmania, tracking their journey as they flip their way to the top.<\/p>\n\n\n

How It Works<\/h2>\n\n\n

Our leaderboard is powered by the points system of the International Flipper Pinball Association (IFPA). As players participate in IFPA-endorsed events within the state, they earn IFPA points based on their performance. These points determine their ranking on our state leaderboard.<\/p>\n\n\n
