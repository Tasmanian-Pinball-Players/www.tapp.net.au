Everyone is Welcome!<\/h2>\n\n\n

At the Tasmanian Association of Pinball Players (TAPP), we believe that the joy of pinball is for everyone. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or have never touched a pinball machine before, we encourage you to come along and join in the fun. In our community, everyone helps each other by sharing tips on gameplay, rules, and techniques. Don’t worry about your skill level \u2013 pinball is all about enjoyment and improvement at your own pace.<\/p>\n\n\n

Volunteer and Contribute<\/h2>\n\n\n

Are you passionate about pinball and eager to contribute more to the community? We are always on the lookout for volunteers to help us with various aspects of running our events and community activities. Whether it’s event organisation, community outreach, or any other way you think you can contribute, we’d love to hear from you.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Sponsorship Opportunities<\/h2>\n\n\n

    For businesses and individuals interested in sponsoring TAPP events or activities, your support is invaluable in helping us grow the pinball community in Tasmania. Sponsorship can take many forms, from supplying prizes, funding events, and to providing equipment or venue space.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    • Explore Sponsorship:<\/strong> Contact Us<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n

      Host Your Own Pinball Tournament<\/h2>\n\n\n

      If you’re interested in hosting your own pinball tournament, we’re here to support you! TAPP welcomes anyone willing to organise pinball tournaments, and we’re happy to list your event on our website, provided it’s open to everyone. Plus, if your tournament meets the requirements set by the International Flipper Pinball Association (IFPA), it can count towards state scoring and finals.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

        \n
      • Organise a Tournament:<\/strong> Contact Us<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n

        Be a Part of Our Community<\/h2>\n\n\n

        By getting involved with TAPP, you’re not just playing a game \u2013 you’re becoming part of a community that shares your passion.
