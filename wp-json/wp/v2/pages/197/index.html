Your Privacy on TAPP’s Website<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Overview<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The Tasmanian Association of Pinball Players (TAPP) is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and tournament participants. Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, primarily designed to provide information with minimal interaction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Data Collection and Use<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • No Cookies or Data Collection on Our Site:<\/strong> TAPP does not use cookies and does not collect or store any personal information about visitors to our website.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • GitHub Pages Hosting:<\/strong> While TAPP does not collect data, our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect some information independently. For details, please review GitHub’s Privacy Statement<\/a>.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Tournament Results and Privacy<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
      \n
    • Publication of Tournament Results:<\/strong> For tournaments, we publish results, including competitors’ names, on the IFPA website, our website, and, in some cases, on matchplay.events. This is a part of the standard process for official pinball tournaments and helps in maintaining accurate and transparent records.\n
        \n
      • Don’t want your details listed? Please contact us<\/a>.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • IFPA and Matchplay.events Privacy Policies:<\/strong> We encourage our participants to review the privacy policies of these platforms to understand how their data is handled:\n
          \n
        • IFPA Privacy Policy<\/a><\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • Matchplay.events Privacy Policy<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

          Limited Interaction on Our Website<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
            \n
          • Minimal Interactive Features:<\/strong> Our website is designed to be informative, with limited interactive features. Thus, there are minimal opportunities for data collection or tracking.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

            Questions and Concerns<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
