What is TazPin?<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

TazPin is an annual celebration that honours the rich history and vibrant future of pinball machines. Returning for its third year, TazPin 2024 is set to take place on the weekend of August 3 \u2013 4, continuing the success that began with its inaugural event in 2022. This extraordinary gathering is the brainchild of dedicated volunteers who share a deep passion for pinball and are eager to spread this love to others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The TazPin Experience<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • Over 50 Pinball Machines:<\/strong> Thanks to the generosity of private collectors, attendees will have the chance to play on over 50 different pinball machines, spanning both classic and modern eras. It’s a unique opportunity to experience a wide array of pinball history under one roof.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Pinball Expo:<\/strong> TazPin serves as an exhibition aimed at introducing newcomers to the wonders of pinball, while also providing seasoned players a chance to meet, play, and experience a wider variety of machines.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Community and Connection:<\/strong> More than just a gaming event, TazPin is a hub for pinball enthusiasts to meet and bond over their shared passion. It’s a welcoming and inclusive space where you can exchange stories, share tips, and perhaps forge new friendships.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • A Weekend of Fun:<\/strong> TazPin isn’t just for die-hard pinball fans; it’s an ideal weekend activity for anyone looking for enjoyment and a sense of community. It’s an invitation to dive into the world of pinball and join a gathering of like-minded individuals.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Don’t Miss the Fun!<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

    TazPin is more than a pinball event; it’s a celebration of a beloved pastime and a vibrant community. Don’t miss this amazing opportunity to immerse yourself in the thrill of pinball and connect with fellow enthusiasts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Join us at TazPin 2024 for an unforgettable weekend \u2013 we can’t wait to welcome you to our pinball family!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
