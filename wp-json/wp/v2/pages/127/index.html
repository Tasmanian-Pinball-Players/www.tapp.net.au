Fortnightly Pinball Evenings<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The Tasmanian Association of Pinball Players (TAPP) is excited to invite you to our regular pinball meetups in Hobart. Starting from Friday, the 23rd of February 2024, we\u2019ll be gathering approximately every two weeks for an evening of pinball, camaraderie, and friendly competition.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Whilst we get established, the exact night, time, and location will vary. Make sure to pay attention to our upcoming events page and social media to keep up to date!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Visit our upcoming events<\/a> page to see all the details.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Event Format and Kickoff<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We start gathering at 6:00 PM where the rules and format will be explained. If you arrive too late you may be unable to join the competition or receive negative points for the missed matches.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Venue and Schedule<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Initially, our meetups will take place at Replay Bar in the Hobart Mall. However, we plan to keep things fresh and engaging by periodically changing the location and possibly the specific day and time for future events. We\u2019ll always aim to keep the meetups approximately two weeks apart.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Stay Informed<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Changes to the location, day, and time of future events will be announced on our website, through our social media channels, and on the IFPA calendar<\/a>. We’ll provide at least 30 days’ notice for any changes to ensure you can plan ahead.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

All Are Welcome<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to the world of pinball, our meetups are open to everyone. It’s a fantastic opportunity to play, learn, and meet other pinball enthusiasts in a relaxed and friendly setting.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
