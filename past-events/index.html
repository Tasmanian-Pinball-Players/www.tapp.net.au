Best Game!

Location

Replay Bar

Qualifying results

Best Game

Finals results

2 Strike Knockout

IFPA

Yes

Thank you to everyone who joined us for a fantastic night of pinball at Replay Bar!

For qualifying, all players played on 7 different pinball machines. Points were awarded based on the best scores relative to other players on each machine, with a maximum of 100 points per machine. Only the top 5 scores out of the 7 machines were counted, so players had a chance to recover even if they made mistakes on 2 machines.

After qualifying, we broke into two groups. The top 6 competed for the title, while a fun B-Division played for prizes. The finals were conducted as a 2-strike knockout, where players competed head-to-head, earning a strike for each loss. The last player standing without two strikes was declared the winner.

Congratulations to the winners:

  1. ü•á Simon Voigt
